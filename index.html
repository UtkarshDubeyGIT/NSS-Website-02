About Us
WHAT WE DO
Over the years NSS NSUT Cell has executed initiatives ranging from Camps, Drives to Community welfare projects. NSS NSUT Cell has implemented projects to work in a variety of domains involving Education, Mental health, and Skills with an exemplary work record which, ipso-facto, has been awarded appreciation over the years. It gives volunteers immense pride to implement their skills whether on the technical front or the design and witness them making an impact in the community. NSS NSUT Cell strives to upkeep the legacy of over 52 years rendering the service to the nation, living up to the motto- "Not me But you."

OUR VISION
NSS NSUT Cell is a team of highly motivated young individuals ready to devote all it's resources to meet the social development goals of India in different aspects. NSS has been shaping young leaders, carving their personalities with various ventures which provide them exposure to the community and the opportunity to work for its betterment in domains such as education and healthcare. These projects develop the all-round personality of a volunteer. They get the opportunity to work in content writing, graphic designing, acting and much more apart from delivering social service on ground in villages. The volunteers also feel the satisfaction of working for a good cause and making a positive impact on the world,reflecting our motto
